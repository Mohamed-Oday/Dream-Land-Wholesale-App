<objective>
## Goal
Add low stock alerts on the owner dashboard, a stock adjustment screen for manual corrections, and a stock movement history screen — completing the Stock & Inventory feature set.

## Purpose
The owner needs proactive alerts when products run low, the ability to manually correct stock (initial setup, shrinkage, counting errors), and a full history of all stock changes for accountability. This completes v0.2 milestone.

## Output
- Migration 014: adjust_stock RPC function
- Low stock threshold config on product form
- Dashboard: Low Stock Alerts section (products below threshold)
- Stock adjustment screen (manual corrections with reason)
- Stock movement history screen (per-product movement log)
</objective>
